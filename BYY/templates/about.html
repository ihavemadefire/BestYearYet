What is Best Year Yet?


Best Year Yet (BYY) is my way of sharing my progress toward my 2019 goals

Everyone has a vision for how their life could be better. A promotion, stronger relationships with family and friends and better physique. Maybe finally mastering Spanish, finishing your novel, or dusting off some old hobby that has fallen by the wayside of an active adult life. The New Year often causes people to refect on where they are in their path and what could be better. This invariably leads to a short list of resolutions that are more often mocked than adheared to. Often a resolution is just a wish that we quickly wrap in a thin petina of seriousness. "No, Seriously. This is it. This is my year..." More often than not, resolutions fail becaus they were never more than a wish made with the addtional intensity that comes from the reflective mood of the New Year.

Real progress will take more than a wish,


It requires a plan.

What does one need to turn resolutions into results? Reaching goals requires 4 core things: a S.M.A.R.T goal, a plan for each goal, dedication, a way to record, share, and engage with your results. The last of these is where Best Year Yet comes in. This site allows me to record and share my progess on all my various resolutions. The site is broken into a few different sections.

  • The Home page contains any news of progress I've made in the last month.

  • The Blog is, well, a blog, meant to record my thoughts on my progress and achievements.

  • The Resolutions page will display the resolutions and their progress as well as some high-level metrics.

  • The Books page is place for me to record the deatils of and my thoughts on the books I read this year.

  • The Health page with generate a graphic interface for my various health realted goals for this year.

The task is daunting, I know. I will likely not acchieve them all. Some are more critical that others. Some are included as reach goals. However, the more challenges undertaken, the more victories to be celebrated.

Note:

A significant portion of my career/skillset goals are Computer Science and Coding related. Creating this page is one of those resolutions. As my web dev skills grow throughout the year, this page will grow, change, and improve.
